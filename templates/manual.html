üéØ Title Generation

Generate engaging, SEO-optimized YouTube titles that maximize click-through rates and discoverability.

Step-by-Step Process

1Input Content

Enter your video content, script, or topic description. Provide as much detail as possible for better title suggestions.

2Generate Titles

Click "Generate Titles" and wait for processing (typically 5-15 seconds).

3Review Results

  • Listicle: Number-based format titles
  • Problem-Solution: Addresses specific issues
  • Authority/Expert: Establishes credibility
  • Curiosity-driven: Creates intrigue
  • Time-sensitive: Creates urgency
  • Comparison: Versus/comparison format
  • General engaging: Broad appeal titles

4Use Generated Titles

  • Copy individual titles with one click
  • Copy all titles for comparison
  • Export data as text file
  • Use directly for video upload

üéôÔ∏è Audio Transcription

Convert audio files to text with high accuracy, supporting multiple languages and providing intelligent summaries.

  • German (DE, AT)

  • Portuguese (PT, BR)

Process Overview

  1. Upload Audio: Select your audio file (MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG)
  2. Configure Settings: Choose language or enable auto-detection
  3. Process: Wait for transcription (1-3 minutes per hour of audio)
  4. Review Results: Get transcription with confidence scores
  5. Export: Copy text or download as file
üí° Audio Quality Tips:

  • Speak clearly at consistent pace

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Issue: Generic or irrelevant titles
Solution: Provide more detailed, specific content input

Issue: Poor engagement scores
Solution: Include emotional keywords and specific benefits

Issue: Low accuracy transcription
Solution: Use higher quality audio files, reduce background noise

Issue: Wrong language detection
Solution: Manually select language instead of auto-detection

Issue: Authentication errors
Solution: Verify YouTube API credentials and OAuth setup

Issue: Upload failures
Solution: Check file format, size, and internet connection
